"Deadbug" style construction

Deadbugging is a construction style where individual components are soldered together directly. This allows for an artistic approach towards creating circuits.

This is a battery-powered avalanche pulse generator. Its signature fast-rising pulses are useful in testing instruments and connectors when you need a fast response.

ArxivGraph

Graph search for academic articles on http://arxiv.org.

Pictured above is a citation graph of ~2000 research papers in physics. Each article is a circle. The bigger the circle, the more often it has been cited by other articles. You can see which articles are connected to each other through citation by looking at the connecting lines.
